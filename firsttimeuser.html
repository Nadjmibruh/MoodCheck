Welcome to MoodCheck — your personal space for mental wellness.

University life can be overwhelming, with assignments, exams, and daily pressures affecting your mental health. MoodCheck is a safe, student-friendly platform designed to help you understand and improve your emotional well-being. Through guided assessments, personalized self-care activities, and easy access to UTeM's support services, MoodCheck empowers you to take control of your mental health — one small step at a time.
